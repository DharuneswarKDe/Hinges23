  • Players take turns to remove a block from a tower and balance it on top, creating a taller and increasingly unstable structure as the game progresses.

  • Team which has construct the highest tower is announced as winner.

  • Based on accuracy of one minute winner will be announced.

Run On Rings

  • Multi-player game consists of THREE in a team.
  • Start run in the series of rings and opposite team player faces off.
  • To move forward play (stone-paper-scissor) who will reaches another end of the rings announced as a winner.
