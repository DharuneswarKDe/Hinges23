About SASTRA

Founded in 1984 as Shanmuga College of Engineering, SASTRA University ranks amongst the most eminent educational institutions of our country. Since its inception SASTRA has achieved world standards in academics, extracurricular and co-curricular activities.On receiving the prestigious accreditation of a GRADE 'A++' University by the NAAC Committee, the credentials of SASTRA have scaled greater heights

About Hinges

Hinges is the annual National Technical Symposium of School of Civil Engineering at SASTRA. Over the past years, Hinges has been nurtured by students and professors alike and has been immensely successful due to their very personal and deep involvement with it. The passion and micro-management shown during the previous years has helped Hinges carve a niche for itself, as the biggest and most successful departmental symposium SASTRA has ever witnessed.

  • Pioneering a Legacy of Excellence

    HINGES stands as one of the oldest and most successful events in our institution's esteemed legacy.

  • Empowering Minds, Transforming Futures

    HINGES serves as a dynamic platform where aspiring engineers, visionaries, and industry leaders

  • An Event Connecting Boundaries

    HINGES has curated and delivered over 60 productive events, including thought-provoking discussions, cutting-edge workshops, and hands-on technical challenges
